# Vidalia Onion Dip

## Ingredients
* 8 oz cream cheese
* 1/2 cup mayonaise
* 1 cup vidalia onion, finely chopped
* 1/4 cup parmesan cheese
* 1+ tsp Frank's Red Hot
* salt
* pepper
* paprika

## Directions
1. Mix together, and spread in a shallow baking dish.
1. Sprinkle with paprika.
1. Bake for 40 minutes at 350F.
