Inspired by [this recipe](https://choosingchia.com/15-minute-sesame-ginger-noodles/) but with extra ingredients.

## Stir Fry Ingredients
- 1 lb groud pork (we use turkey lately)
- 3 medium carrots, thinly sliced into coins or half moons
- 4 bok choy bunches, chopped into 1 inch pieces
- 2 green onions (or half a regular onion), thinly sliced
- 300g dry rice noodles or 600g fresh/pre-copked udon noodles
- optional: sesame seeds, crushed red chillis

## Sauce Ingredients
- 4 cloves of garlic, minced
- 4 tbsp fresh minced ginger
- 1/3 cup soy sauce
- 3 tbsp maple syrup
- 1 tbsp sesame oil

## Directions
1. Mix the sauce ingredients together in a bowl and set aside
2. Optional: toast the sesame seeds in a dry pan, and pour into a bowl or plate for later
3. In a large wok or frying pan, cook the carrots in a bit of olive and sesame oil until tender (about 7 minutes)
4. If using regular onion, add it now
5. Add the ground turkey to the pan (or with pork, cook separately and drain)
6. Add the reserved sauce to the carrots, and simmer for 2 minutes
7. Add the pork, bok choy, and green onions, and cook until tenter (about 8 minutes)
8. Meanwhile, cook the noodles according to their instructions
9. When the bok choy is done, mix the noodles in and stir until coated
10. Serve, sprinkled with sesame seeds and crushed chillis if desired
